As a husband, dog owner, researcher, occasional homeowner, sometimes student, and fitful writer, I like to take on more than is probably wise. This page links to some of my ongoing projects, both personal and professional.

Collaboration Stats

Begun in Summer of 2009 as a contract project for the Wikimedia Foundation’s Strategic Planning process, CollabStats is a collection of tools to mine IRC chat logs and MediaWiki dumps and answer questions about process. Questions like, “Who’s involved?”, “What are they doing?”, and “When do they do it?”, as well as, “How many people care about this thing?”

The Heart Sutra Project (Stalled; Incomplete)

Unfortunately, life intervened and the project was never completed. I’d like to request permission again and finish this some day.
